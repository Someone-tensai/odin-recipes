Pesto Pasta Recipe

Home

Description

Pesto pasta is simple to make and full of flavor. This recipe is perfect for busy weeknights and comes together in just 15 minutes. Use any pasta, and add as much pesto sauce as you like. Enjoy topped with plenty of Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil your favorite pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the basil pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
